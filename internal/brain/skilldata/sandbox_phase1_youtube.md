# Sandbox Phase 1 — YouTube 10 Channel Validate

## When to Use
- Kickoff scale 200 channel project
- Validate formula sebelum scale Phase 12+ (30 → 100 → 200)

## Steps

### Day 1-3: Channel Setup
1. Bikin 10 channel via Donut Browser:
   - 4 sleep music channel
   - 3 piano office channel
   - 2 Christian worship channel
   - 1 Christmas variant (test Q4 prep)

2. Naming: pakai hook word formula (yt.md Bagian 15)
   - Sleep: "Deep Sleep Vault", "Healing Sleep Sanctuary", dll
   - Piano: "Deep Work Piano", "Calm Office Piano", dll

3. Polish channel page:
   - Banner 2560x1440 visual match niche
   - About: 1 paragraf value prop + upload schedule + email business
   - Playlist minimal 3 tematik
   - Channel trailer 15-30 detik

### Day 4-6: Production Batch
1. Suno generate 30 candidate (3 per channel)
2. Demucs separate stem batch
3. Reaper remix workflow per `suno_music_production.md`
4. Output 10 WAV unique

### Day 7: Upload Wave 1
1. 1 video per channel via Donut Browser MCP
2. Title English specific (yt.md Bagian 4 formula)
3. Description 2 baris keyword-rich
4. Tags 5-10 relevant
5. Country target US/UK/AU

### Day 8-14: Paid Catalyst
1. Google Ads TrueView Discovery
2. $5/hari × 7 hari × 3 channel terbaik = $105 budget
3. Track Studio Analytics daily

### Day 15-30: Decision
- CTR >4% + retention >50% di 14 hari = WINNER → Phase 12 scale
- CTR <2% konsisten 30 hari = KILL → build ulang nama + fingerprint
- Mid range = iterate thumbnail + title

## Success Metric
- Min 2 channel WINNER
- Min 100 subscriber per channel dalam 30 hari
- Cost per subscriber <$0.50
